Miso Soup

History of Miso Soup

Miso soup has been a fundamental part of Japanese meals for centuries. Made from fermented soybean paste, it is valued for its deep umami flavor and nutritional benefits. It is often served as a daily accompaniment to meals.

How to Make Miso Soup

Ingredients

  • Miso paste
  • Dashi stock
  • Tofu cubes
  • Seaweed (wakame)
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Heat dashi stock in a pot.
  2. Add tofu and seaweed.
  3. Turn off heat and dissolve miso paste.
  4. Garnish with green onions and serve.
