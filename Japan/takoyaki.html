Takoyaki

History of Takoyaki

Takoyaki originated in Osaka and is a popular Japanese street food. These bite-sized octopus balls are cooked in a special pan and topped with sauce, mayonnaise, and bonito flakes.

How to Make Takoyaki

Ingredients

  • Takoyaki batter
  • Octopus pieces
  • Green onions
  • Takoyaki sauce
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Heat takoyaki pan and pour in batter.
  2. Add octopus and onions.
  3. Turn balls until round and golden.
  4. Top with sauce and mayonnaise.
