Okonomiyaki

History of Okonomiyaki

Okonomiyaki is a savory Japanese pancake that originated in Osaka. Its name means “grilled as you like it,” reflecting the customizable nature of the dish with various fillings and toppings.

How to Make Okonomiyaki

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Pork slices
  • Okonomiyaki sauce

Steps

  1. Mix batter with cabbage and eggs.
  2. Cook on a pan until golden.
  3. Add pork and flip.
  4. Top with sauce and serve.
