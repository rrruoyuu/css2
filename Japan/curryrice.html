Curry Rice

Japanese curry rice (カレーライス) is a hearty and comforting dish, loved for its rich, mild, and slightly sweet curry sauce served over fluffy white rice.

Adapted from Indian curry via the British Navy in the 19th century, it has become a staple home-cooked meal and a favorite in schools and restaurants across Japan.

Curry Rice – Home Recipe

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked Japanese rice
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 potato, chopped
  • 200g chicken, beef, or pork

Curry Sauce

  • 1 box Japanese curry roux
  • 2 cups water
  • Cooking oil

How to Make Curry Rice (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Sauté the Aromatics

  • Heat oil in a pot and sauté onions until soft and fragrant.

Step 2: Cook the Meat

  • Add meat and cook until browned on all sides.

Step 3: Add Vegetables and Simmer

  • Add chopped carrots and potatoes.
  • Pour in water and bring to a simmer for 15 minutes until vegetables are tender.

Step 4: Add Curry Roux

  • Turn off the heat and add the curry roux.
  • Stir until the roux is fully dissolved.
  • Simmer again for 10 minutes until thickened.

Step 5: Serve

  • Serve the curry hot over steamed rice.

Tip: For extra flavor, you can add apple, honey, or soy sauce to the curry sauce to enhance sweetness and depth.
