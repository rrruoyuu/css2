Chilli Crab

Chilli Crab is one of Singapore’s most famous seafood dishes and is widely recognised as a national icon. Created in Singapore, this dish features fresh crab cooked in a thick, savoury-sweet chilli-based sauce made using tomato-based ingredients, chilli, and spices, resulting in a rich and flavourful gravy.

Chilli Crab is traditionally enjoyed with steamed or fried mantou buns, which are used to soak up the delicious sauce. Commonly served at seafood restaurants and hawker stalls, this dish is loved for its bold flavours, slightly spicy kick, and satisfying combination of tender crab meat and rich sauce, making it a must-try for locals and visitors alike.

Chilli Crab - Home Recipe

Ingredients

Main Ingredient

  • Live mud crab – 1.5 kg (3 lb), cut into large pieces
  • Crab tomalley (“crab mustard”) – reserved

Chilli Paste

  • Red cayenne chillies – 5, roughly chopped
  • Taucu paste (fermented soy paste) – 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic – 6 cloves, roughly chopped
  • Eschalots – 2 small, roughly chopped
  • Ginger – 3 tablespoons, roughly chopped

Singapore Chilli Crab Sauce

  • Canola oil – ¼ cup
  • Belachan (dried shrimp paste) – 1 tablespoon, chopped
  • Tomato passata – 1 cup
  • Sweet chilli sauce – ½ cup
  • Ketchup – 2 tablespoons
  • Water – 2 cups
  • Egg – 1, lightly whisked
  • White vinegar – 3 tablespoons

Garnishes / Serving

  • Fresh coriander
  • Green onion – 2 tablespoons, finely sliced
  • Golden mantou buns

How to Make Singapore Chilli Crab (Step-by-Step)

Part A: Prepare the Crab

  • Step 1: Prepare the Crab
    • Clean and cut the crab into large pieces.
    • Reserve the tomalley (“crab mustard”) and all crab juices.

Part B: Make the Chilli Paste

  • Step 2: Blend Chilli Paste
    • Place all chilli paste ingredients into a food processor.
    • Blend until a fairly smooth paste forms.

Part C: Cook the Sauce

  • Step 3: Sauté Belachan
    • Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    • Add belachan and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  • Step 4: Cook Chilli Paste
    • Add the chilli paste to the pot.
    • Cook for about 3 minutes, stirring continuously.
  • Step 5: Add Tomato Passata
    • Add tomato passata.
    • Cook for about 4 minutes, stirring regularly.
  • Step 6: Add Remaining Sauce Ingredients
    • Add sweet chilli sauce, ketchup, and water.
    • Stir well to combine.

Part D: Cook the Crab

  • Step 7: Cook Crab Claws
    • Add crab claws to the sauce first.
    • Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
    • Cover and cook for 3 minutes.
  • Step 8: Add Tomalley and Remaining Crab
    • Add reserved tomalley and crab juices.
    • Add remaining crab pieces.
    • Cover and cook for about 7 minutes.

Part E: Finish the Sauce

  • Step 9: Create Egg Ribbons
    • Remove crab pieces from the pot.
    • While stirring the sauce, slowly pour in the whisked egg.
    • Stir to form egg ribbons.
  • Step 10: Add Vinegar
    • Stir vinegar into the sauce.
  • Step 11: Return Crab
    • Return crab pieces to the pot.
    • Gently stir to coat with sauce.

Part F: Serve

  • Step 12: Plate and Serve
    • Transfer crab to a serving dish.
    • Pour all sauce over the crab.
    • Garnish with coriander and green onion.
    • Serve with golden mantou buns.

Tip: The key to a good Singapore Chilli Crab lies in the sauce—using fresh crab, reserved crab tomalley (“crab mustard”), and allowing the sauce to simmer properly creates a rich, savoury, and slightly sweet gravy that perfectly coats the crab.
